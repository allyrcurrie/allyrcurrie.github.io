Welcome to Futura.

Scroll down for an in-depth overview of this project.

This project was created for ARTS 303, Graphic Design I. The goal of this project was to research a specific font type, and then create a magazine spread (including cover) for the chosen font. I decided to focus on Futura, a font released in 1927 by Paul Renner. While many depictions of Futura include red and black due to its German origin, I decided to go with bright neons such as pink, yellow, and blues as my base colors to give it a more futuristic feel. I was heavily inspired by Swiss graphic design work, such as Hans Neuburg or Massimo Vignelli.

To start our project, we created a moodboard to establish the look of our desired magazine aesthetic. I pulled many images of swiss design while also establishing the font type and color palette chosen in the middle.
